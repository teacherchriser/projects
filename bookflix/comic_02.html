The Last Of The Mohicans

Action · Drama · Historical

In the midst of the French and Indian War, a rugged frontiersman and his Mohican companions fight to protect two British sisters from deadly enemies. As cultures clash and loyalties are tested, an epic journey of love, survival, and sacrifice unfolds in the wild American frontier. Based on James Fenimore Cooper's gripping novel.

Made by: Emily Rodriguez - Miguel Torres - Andrea Garces - Diana Pena - Jessica Florez - ARW 2025-1

Directed by: Teacher Christian Serrano
