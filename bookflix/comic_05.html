The Call Of The Wild

Adventure · Drama · Survival

Stolen from his comfortable home and thrust into the harsh Yukon wilderness, a loyal dog named Buck must learn to survive—and discover his true nature. As he adapts to life in the wild, a powerful bond with a kind-hearted man leads to a journey of freedom and self-discovery. Based on Jack London’s timeless classic.

Made by: Luisa Carrero - Isis Artunduaga - Maria Fernanda Rozo - ARW 2025-1

Directed by: Teacher Christian Serrano
