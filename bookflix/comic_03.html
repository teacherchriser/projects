Moby Dick

Adventure · Drama · Psychological

Obsessed with revenge, Captain Ahab leads his crew on a perilous voyage across the oceans to hunt the legendary white whale that maimed him. Through the eyes of young sailor Ishmael, this gripping tale explores madness, fate, and the power of nature. Based on Herman Melville’s epic novel.
