Gulliver Travels

Adventure · Fantasy · Satire

When a curious Englishman sets sail on a voyage across the seas, he finds himself shipwrecked in strange lands full of tiny people, talking horses, and giants. As he journeys through bizarre and fantastical worlds, he questions everything he thought he knew about society, power, and human nature. Based on Jonathan Swift's classic satire, this story blends adventure with sharp social commentary.

Made by: Ana Cordoba - Tatiana Parra - Daniel Huertas - Gabriela Martinez - ARW 2025-1

Directed by: Teacher Christian Serrano
